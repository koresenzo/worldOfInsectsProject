Wasp Anatomy by Enzo Flores

Annotated figure of a wasp showing different body parts.

  • Head - The head of the wasp contains the eyes, antennae, and mouthparts.
  • Thorax - The thorax is the middle part of the wasp's body, where the wings and legs are attached.
  • Abdomen - The abdomen is the rear part of the wasp's body, containing the digestive and reproductive organs.
  • Wings - The wasp has two pairs of wings that are used for flying.
  • Legs - The wasp has six legs that are used for walking and gripping surfaces.
